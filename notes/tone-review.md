# Tone Review

The first version of the BS Engineering repository was structurally useful, but too dry.

This note captures the editorial correction:

- more natural language,
- less generic wording,
- clearer personal motivation,
- stronger explanation of why each project exists,
- better balance between professional structure and human voice.

The goal is not to make the documents emotional in an artificial way. The goal is to make them feel written by a real person with a real technical journey.
