# Vision

BS Engineering is a long-term project about becoming easier to understand as an engineer.

For years, my experience has moved through different technical areas: hardware, IT, web technologies, manufacturing, metrology, quality, documentation, data and systems thinking. The goal of BS Engineering is to connect those pieces into one clear story instead of leaving them as disconnected job titles and skills.

## The direction

I want BS Engineering to grow into a professional portfolio that shows not only what I know, but also how I think.

The long-term vision is to build a credible engineering identity around one central idea:

> I like understanding complex systems, finding practical improvements and connecting physical processes with digital tools.

That idea fits my experience in manufacturing quality, measurement systems, technical documentation, IT support, Linux, web technologies and process improvement.

## What this should become

Over time, this repository should become:

- a clear map of my professional direction,
- a home for well-documented engineering projects,
- a source of truth for resume and LinkedIn updates,
- a record of learning and development,
- a place where future employers can see consistency, curiosity and technical discipline.

## Desired impression

When someone reads this material, I do not want them to see a collection of random interests.

I want them to see a person who:

- takes technical work seriously,
- documents ideas carefully,
- learns continuously,
- understands both physical and digital systems,
- values quality, traceability and clarity,
- prefers honest competence over inflated claims.

The vision is simple: make BS Engineering a professional, human and trustworthy representation of my engineering journey.
