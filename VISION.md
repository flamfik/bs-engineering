# Vision

BS Engineering exists to become a long-term professional engineering brand built on credibility, documentation and continuous improvement.

The vision is to create a coherent ecosystem where resume, LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio, design system and learning roadmap all communicate the same professional identity:

> An engineering and technology professional who understands complex systems, improves technical processes and connects manufacturing, quality, data and software.

## Long-term direction

Over the next years, BS Engineering should become a recognizable technical portfolio based on:

- well-documented engineering projects,
- honest representation of real skills,
- clear project case studies,
- continuous learning records,
- practical problem solving,
- quality-focused engineering thinking.

## Desired perception

A recruiter, hiring manager or technical lead should understand that this profile represents someone who:

- documents work carefully,
- learns systematically,
- understands both physical and digital systems,
- can support quality and manufacturing environments,
- can communicate technical reasoning clearly,
- values credibility over inflated claims.
